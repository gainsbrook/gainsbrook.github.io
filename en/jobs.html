Vacancies

Gainsbrook SA is hiring an experienced Web Programmer on full time basis. This includes working with our e-commerce website, marketing database development and support, SEO, and content management systems. We maintain a casual dress code and a friendly, high energy work atmosphere. We are searching for a dynamic, competent, intelligent, hard-working and responsible individual to join our international IT team for participation in an interesting innovative travel web project, work as part of a team to design and develop websites and interactive web applications.

No freelance.

We are also looking for a talented and creative Graphic Designer (for web) to join our IT team for working on innovative projects and participating as a key member of our team.

Further, we are looking for a Project Manager for working on the contracts with travel wholesalers and lead negotiations.

Gainsbrook SA is a WEB-development company for international online sales travel market. The company is located in Fribourg (Switzerland).

Job tasks, desired skills and experience:

Web Programmer: (PERL, Python, Ruby-on-Rails): writing templates, scripts. Bachelor's degree in the IT field and 3-5 years of experience in the development of websites; advanced skills in FastCGl, TT, AJAX, Template Toolkit, Catalyst or Mojo; experience with developing database-driven web applications using MySQL.

Graphic Designer for web: develop and design: logos, websites, user interface design, e-mail creatives. Good typography knowledge, graphic design experience. Any software for graphic design (Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, etc.).

Project Manager: making contracts with wholesalers for online sales of travel packages and online hotel booking. Experience in travel industry.

Russian language is a plus.

Casual dress code, 4 weeks vacation in the 1st year. If needed, Swiss working permits are made for Europeans, US citizens and Russians.

Please send your CV to office@gainsbrook.ch and state in the subject line which position you are applying for. Only suitable candidates will be contacted.
