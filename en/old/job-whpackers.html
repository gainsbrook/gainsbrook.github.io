Warehouse Pickers/Packers

Gainsbrook SA is an international fashion clothes trading company. A young but growing business, is seeking a Warehouse Picker/Packer to resort packs of clothes at our new consolidation centre. This position relates to the logistics operating department that is showing continued-growth throughout our global offices. If necesary we provide training the candidate basic import/export knowledge of customs entry processing regimes, according to which we should prepare branded clothes packs and documents.

Job Description:

  • Fashion Clothes Export Operations
  • Resorting of Branded clothes: unpacking, packing, sizing, weightng, ect. and putting all that into a database (to be dispatched abroad)
  • Order picking and packing
  • Taking goods in and checking
  • Product assembly according to brands
  • Responsible for all warehouse packs of goods, ensuring they are kept tidy, clean and organised at all times

Required skills:

  • Well organised
  • Attention for detail & accuracy
  • Good basic knowledge of Microsoft Applications
  • Ability to meet deadlines
  • Ability to work on their own to complete tasks at the highest standard
  • Benefits: Overtime rates, holiday pay, medical insurance
