Our Principles:

1. Exclusive offers. Limited in time offers; daily e-mail alerts to the customers about upcoming sales; high quality and demanded goods. All that makes all offers from club-sale.ru exclusive and popular, and it does not compete with the offline retail sector.

2. We use the category matrix. Goods do not compete while being presented on our website. There are no similar goods of different brands at the same time on club-sale.ru. Only one brand in each category. 16 new offers daily, each lasts for not more than 3 days.

3. Our prices should be the lowest in comparison with other retail places, by this we reach a bigger sales volume in a short period of time.

4. Innovative performance style. E-mail alert way of delivering the information on sales allows us to exhibit hundred times more goods than any other ordinary online store with constant.

5. Unique shopping experience. Our customers are getting a unique shopping experience as well as entertainment by browsing through the assortment of goods we offer. On average, one customer looks through 40-60 web-pages during one session. That is the highest index among online stores.

6. Private shopping-club. Private atmosphere of a special event arouses customer's loyalty to the project and the customers transmit their loyalty onto new brands. The brand image protection is guaranted as there is no conflict with the offline retail sector.

7. We choose long term business relationships. We carry out sales of each successful brand on average once in 1-3 months, reaching good sales results within a year.
