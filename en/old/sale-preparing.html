Sale Preparing

The process of sale preparing in our own production-studio

Receiving samples

  • Club-Sale.ru receives samples
  • We carefully prepare the samples for the photography, steam them and put info into our database
  • We dress models and take pictures of the samples in general and in details (in special photo studio)
  • The main photo center with 5 studios is located in Russia. 1 photo studio is in Switzerland. And an additional photo studio is in Italy.


Models casting

  • There are models on staff. For each brand models are chosen in accordance with the brand’s concept.
  • Brands can offer their own models or existing images
  • Our professional stylists carefully make the looks, make-up and choose accesories


Making photos

  • Professional photographers, assistants, stylists and make up artists take pictures in the company’s professional photo studios.
  • If samples have been photogrphed in the italian sudio, all the images are sent to the russian office for further processing.
  • All photos are improved by the team of retouch artists and color correctors


Design of advertising materials

  • Art directors and designers prepare advertising materials for each sale: photo and video announcements, banners of different formats, e-mail designs, blogs and articles for social networks
  • We are happy to use your own photos and video materials


Content

  • A team of editors prepares all images and advertising materials and the description of the brand’s campaign
  • Copywriters prepare individual image and technical characteristics for every item, describing all the advantages of each product: fabric, color, finishing, fitting, etc.


Final control

  • The Final Control Manager puts together all the materials and sends them to the brand’s representative for the final approval.
  • The supplier confirms the stock and pricing
  • If needed, the samples are returned to the supplier (subject to the contract)


Image concept, brand perception and its positioning are the key values in preparing of the special offers events for Club-sale.ru and Cluberia.ch!
