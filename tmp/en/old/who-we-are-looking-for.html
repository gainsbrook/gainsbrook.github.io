Who we are looking for

Who we are looking for

Gainsbrook is international company selling luxury fashion goods through the internet. Gainsbrook offers a clothes, shoes and accessories on the web sites working in the model of private shopping club.

Now we are searching for the the following positions for our purchasing office in Fribourg:

1. Category Manager .

The main task is making category matrix for purchasing and selling fashion goods.
Requirements: being interested in fashion goods, experience in planning assortment for clothes store, knowing and being experienced in apparel retail market. Fluent English is required.
Responsibilities: making plans for buyers. Analyze consumer buying showings and try to foresee future trends.

2. Buyer of fashion goods.

Requirements: being a fan of fashion, experience in apparel retail buying. Good skills in MS Office. Communication skills. Opportunity to go on business trips. Fluent English and Italianx/French.
Responsibilities: carry on negotiations, make a business trip, make a selection of saleable goods. Buying goods in Italy and Switzerland keeping up to trends. Place re-orders according to the plans of category manager.

3. Buyer assistant.

Requirements: ability to search contacts of partner companies (suppliers). Some marketing experience is preferable. Being fluent in English, Italian/French languages.
Responsibilities: making calls, preliminary talks, management of contracts.

We are eager to see enthusiastic and energetic people in order to create a strong team for innovative internet project. To know more about opened positions or to send us your resume, please use e-mail: office@gainsbrook.ch
