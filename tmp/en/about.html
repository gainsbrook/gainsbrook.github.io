Gainsbrook SA

Swiss company Gainsbrook SA is a part of the Mediamarket Holding Inc. group which is an international group of companies including Internet retailers of branded and luxury goods, warehouse complexes, buying offices and Online Travel provider.

Club-Sale Travel

We apply the concept of short sales to a new tourism project for booking hotels, tickets and travel packages, ready to be presented to 1.1 million active customers.

This is a new project for the Club-Sale Group for registered customers.

Exclusive offers are available with discounts up to 50%. We select the best deals around the world. Most "hot" areas are strongly desired by Russian tourists who seek the best vacation at an affordable price.

Each sale lasts up to 5 days or until all rooms are sold. Such a short period of time and limited supply leaves little time for reflection and lets customers make a positive decision to purchase.

More than 100 employees are involved in the preparation and conduct of each sale. A team of professional content managers (including editors, photographers and web designers) work within the planning of sales campaigns for hotels on our site. We prepare the flash videos of local areas and services, as well as a complete description of the infrastructure. On selected dates for travel, we include airline tickets, tickets for concerts, exhibitions and other cultural events. So we create an exclusive and complete tour package.

Club-Sale Travel is managed by an enthusiastic staff who is passionate about traveling.



MediaMarket Holding Inc.

MediaMarket Holding Inc. is an international group of retailing and purchasing companies dealing with luxury and fashion goods. The main project is an online retailing shopping club www.Club-Sale.ru which processes up to 1 000 000 online orders per year.

Starting its work in 2003 with health and beauty goods, the group of companies was jointed by a new project Club-Sale.ru on the sales of clothes and accessories in 2009 which quickly became a mainstream and replaced all main existing projects. Now, the project www.Ochkov.Net - an on-line (also catalogue) and offline retailer of contact lens, glasses and accessories - is of secondary importance. Other projects were stopped and closed because of insufficient indexes of efficiency.

The head office is situated in Moscow (Russia). Our fulfilment base occupies 5000 sq meters building and allows us to process up to 5000 orders daily. More than 600 specialists are engaged in consistent processes of call-center, delivery department, warehouse, photo studios and content manufacturing.

There are a lot of famous, popular and beloved brands of apparel and accessories, home decor and toys. Our purchasing offices are situated in Russia, Switzerland and Italy. Consolidation and operational warehouses are in Switzerland and Russia. We deal with american, european and russian suppliers and are always in a search of new business partners. The consept of MediaMarket Holding Inc. projects is the maximum assortment, the lowest prices and the best service. That is why we are always aimed at expansion and diversification of the categories of goods and searching of new brands while respecting the most competitive prices and conditions.

The main attention while working with clients is payed to the quality of goods and service for consumers. We are trying to be very flexible so that our consumers don't have any hesitations while placing orders and don't have any regrets after having it delivered.

MediaMarket Holding Inc. takes a strong position in the Russian market, and in addition has a good growth performance. We have a good potential resource for the dynamic growth of our company and the active promotion of new products and brands in Russia.
