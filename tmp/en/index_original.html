Welcome to the corporate site of Gainsbrook SA.

Gainsbrook SA, with headquarters in Switzerland, manages the online shopping club Club-Sale.ru and the new innovative travel project Club-Sale Travel. Both belong to the Club-Sale Group.


Club-Sale.ru is a private shopping club offering the best in luxury fashion and accessories to members-only prices and is one of the leaders of flash sale providers in the Russian market. Club-Sale.ru focuses on fashion clothing and home decorating. The project aims to have the best quality, price level and popularity of the brands on show. And it is successfully achieving its objectives.



Club-Sale Travel is the new project of the Club-Sale Group and aims to provide new and exciting travel packages to world wide destinations at a great price, also in the form of flash sales. Destinations and hotels are chosen to match the taste of our demanding clientele, and have to fulfill all our criteria. When booking a vacation with Club-Sale Travel, the customer can be assured to enjoy a getaway which lives up to his expectations.
