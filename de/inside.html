Projects

CLUB-SALE.ru project was offered on March, 18 in 2009 by our IT and designer teams and it started up a few months later.

CLUB-SALE.ru is a closed shopping club, where registered customers can buy goods online. The goods are available only for a short period. A brand sale takes no more than four days. As the number of goods available is limited and the price is unprecedently low, the most purchases are made during the first day of the sale or even during first hours.

Clothes, accessories, leather accessories, home goods, jewelries, interior items are for sale. The orders are delivered for free in Moscow and St. Petersburg next day. The delivery in Russia usually takes about 2 or 5 days.

This fulfillment base makes it possible to process and realize up to 5000 orders a day. That is why the company enjoys a substantial staff of couriers and large fleet of cars as well as contracts with professional courier services. Our call center embraces 200 operator jobs. Storage facilities of 2000 sq. m are specially equipped for effective storage and processing of the incoming goods. Four photographic studios are designed to make the goods look right and appealing.

The concept of CLUB-SALE.ru development is based on the idea of “maximum range, the lowest prices and the best service”. That is why we are always enlarging the products range, introducing new brands and presenting luring offers form the well-known ones.

We have actually sold the problem of leftovers due to our flexibility, high-level services and low prices. Our customers have no concerns while purchasing and no regrets after the purchase has been made.

Special attention shall be paid to the Wholesale Trade Department. Its policy is not only to get the best options of price and quality but also to make the deal with the maximum benefits for our suppliers. Our objective is the solid trusting long-term partnership. It is pleasure to work with us. We are mostly interested in collections of the well-known brands from previous seasons (though no later than a year). The collections shall be presented in a wide and complete size range. First of all we consider rejected and unredeemed items of the present collections subjected to discounts.

Our partners supply the brand goods for sale via this club with the discounts of up to 80% regarding the principles, such as short-term price offer (several days), confidentiality (only registered members of the club are informed) without open exposure, thorough implementation of any region agreements on the goods sale made by the supplier, that has been made earlier with a third party. Implementation of these principles makes it possible to avoid the threat of retail trading.

Each sale is carried out according to individual conditions of the supplier. The exclusive sales require designing a separate site, providing model casting or the mannequin choice, directing an image trailer and a photo gallery according to the CLUB-SALE.ru concept and the concept of the brand owner.

Currently the shop's site reach comprises 500 000 people and it tends to grow steadily. About 2500 orders are processed every day.
