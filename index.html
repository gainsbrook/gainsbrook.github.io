Gainsbrook SA creates and manages e-commerce projects inside and outside Switzerland. Our specialization is niche web-shops aiming to become leader in its sector due to high-end web-design, comprehensive functionality and content, as well as SEO. Besides web-shops Gainsbrook SA also developed and created a booking engine having its core as innovative semantic search of the lowest prices for the hotels all over the world. Being successfully tested by us the booking engine platform can be used as customized private label both for existing travel agencies or start-ups.

Starting with clothing online retail in 2010 (including exporting of textile products) Gainsbrook SA had undergone multiple changes improving the usability of its web-sites, types of sales, expanding the range of services for the customers. All this formed a good experience in online commerce making it possible to produce high-quality websites, online shops and complex technological solutions. Our company can be characterized as dynamic and easy transforming depending on current external conditions. With each new project we are plunged into a new topic putting all our time, expertise and skills.

What never changes is our principles of work – respecting
our partners and loving our clients.
